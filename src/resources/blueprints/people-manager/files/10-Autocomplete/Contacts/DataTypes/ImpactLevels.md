# Unsatisfactory
- Description: Did not meet most or all goals and expectations.

# Needs Improvement
- Description: Met most goals and expectations over the year but improvement needed to meet all.

# Successful
- Description: Consistently met and at times exceeded all goals and expectations.

# Outstanding
- Description: Consistently exceeded all complex goals and expectations; proactively expanded scope or influence to drive broader results.

# Transformational
- Description: Far exceeded the most ambitious and challenging goals and expectations; proactively identified opportunities that impacted business trajectory.
