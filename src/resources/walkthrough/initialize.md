## Initialize Your Workspace

Get started in three steps:

1. Open the **Command Palette** (`Ctrl+Shift+P`)
2. Run **Memoria: Initialize workspace**
3. Pick a template:

| Template | Best for |
|----------|----------|
| **Individual Contributor Notebook** | Developers, PMs, individual work |
| **People Manager Notebook** | Managers with 1:1s, team contacts, project status |

Memoria creates the folder structure, starter files, and configuration — ready to use immediately.
