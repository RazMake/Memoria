## Explore Your Knowledge Base

After initialization, open the **Explorer** panel to see your new structure.

Each top-level folder has a clear purpose:

| Folder | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| `00-ToDo/` | Active tasks and action items |
| `01-People/` | Team member notes *(Manager template)* |
| `01-ToRemember/` or `02-ToRemember/` | Important reference notes |
| `02-MeetingNotes/` or `03-MeetingNotes/` | Meeting records and 1:1s |
| `03-Inbox/` or `04-Inbox/` | Quick capture for incoming items |
| `04-Archive/` or `05-Archive/` | Completed or retired items |

Folders are numbered for consistent ordering in the Explorer.

> **Tip:** If **Explorer Decorations** are enabled, folders have color-coded badges to help you navigate at a glance.
