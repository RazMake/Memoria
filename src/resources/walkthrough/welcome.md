## Welcome to Memoria 👋

Memoria helps you build a **personal knowledge base** right inside VS Code.

Instead of scattered notes across apps and folders, Memoria scaffolds an organized structure in your workspace — with dedicated folders for:

- ✅ **TODOs** — Track tasks and action items
- 📝 **Meeting Notes** — Keep records of meetings and 1:1s
- 📂 **Inbox** — Capture quick thoughts and incoming items
- 🗄️ **Archive** — Move completed items out of the way

Choose a **blueprint template** that matches your role, and Memoria sets everything up for you.
